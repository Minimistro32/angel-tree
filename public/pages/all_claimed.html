Unfamiliar with Venmo?

  1. Set Up a Venmo Account:
    If you don't already have a Venmo account, download the Venmo app from your mobile app store or visit the Venmo website. Sign up for an account and link it to your bank account or credit/debit card.
  2. Open Venmo:
    Launch the Venmo app on your mobile device or visit the Venmo website.
  3. Initiate a Payment:
    In the Venmo app, tap on the "Pay or Request" button, or on the website, click on "Pay or Request" from the main menu.
  4. Enter "@givingtree_jalynn":
    In the "To" field, type in our Venmo username "@givingtree_jalynn" as the recipient.
  5. Enter the Donation Amount:
    In the "Amount" field, enter the amount you wish to donate.
  6. Choose Your Payment Method:
    Select your preferred payment method, either your linked bank account or credit/debit card.
  7. Review and Confirm:
    Double-check the details to ensure they are correct. Then, tap the "Pay" button to confirm the donation.
