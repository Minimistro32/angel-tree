Venmo Instructions

  1. Set Up Your Own Venmo Account: If you don't already have a Venmo account, download the Venmo app from your mobile app store or visit the Venmo website. Sign up for an account and link it to your bank account or credit/debit card.
  2. Find the Recipient's Venmo Username: You'll need to know the Venmo username or email address of the person or organization you want to donate to. They must also have a Venmo account.
  3. Open the Venmo App: Launch the Venmo app on your mobile device or visit the Venmo website in your web browser.
  4. Initiate a Payment: In the Venmo app, tap on the "Pay or Request" button, or on the website, click on "Pay or Request" from the main menu.
  5. Enter the Recipient's Username or Email: In the "To" field, type in the Venmo username or email address of the recipient to whom you want to donate.
  6. Enter the Donation Amount: In the "Amount" field, enter the amount you wish to donate.
  7. Add a Note (Optional): You can include a note or a message to specify the purpose of your donation. For example, you can mention it's for a charity, fundraiser, or any other specific cause.
  8. Choose Your Payment Method: Select your preferred payment method, either your linked bank account or credit/debit card.
  9. Review and Confirm: Double-check the details to ensure they are correct. Then, tap the "Pay" button to confirm the donation.
  10. Review Transaction: After confirming, you will see a summary of the transaction. Verify the details one last time, and if everything looks correct, you can proceed to complete the transaction.
  11. Transaction Confirmation: Once the transaction is complete, you and the recipient will receive a notification confirming the donation.
